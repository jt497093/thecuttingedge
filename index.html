ABOUT

Fashion production built on trust, quality and international experience.

The Cutting Edge is a fashion production company focused on high-quality manufacturing in China, with a specialization in jackets and denim.

We support brands from concept to final product, including design development, tech packs, sampling, sourcing and full-scale production.

Production takes place through trusted partner factories in China while communication and project management are handled from our office in The Netherlands.
